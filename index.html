Overview

For our final project, we created a LED lighting system using an MSP430G2553. The user plays audio through the aux input, and the LEDs light up in response to the volume levels of different frequency ranges in the audio. To learn more about the details of this project, you can view our project report below.

Parts List

  • 1x msp430g2553 Micro Processor
  • 3x ADA4098-1 Op-Amps
  • 1x Aux Connector
  • 1x PAM8302A Audio Amplifier
  • 1x Speaker
  • 1x MAX619CPA+ Voltage Regulator
  • Resistors: 32k, 53k, 630, 4.7k, 10k, 100k
  • Capacitors: 0.1uF, 0.01uF, 2x 0.015uF,2x 1uF, 1nF, 2x 0.22uF

Usage

To use our board, simply play audio through our aux connector and watch the LEDs light up. To toggle between lighting modes, press the button, and to change the volume of the speaker, use the potentiometer.
